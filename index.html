C++ Car & Player Physics Game

Download

Built with my custom C++ engine using Bullet Physics, this game starts with a minimal main menu featuring two buttons: Play and Exit. The background showcases a procedurally generated terrain with shader-based coloring. When Play is pressed, the menu disappears and the player spawns alongside a drivable car. The player is a third-person animated character who can walk, jump, and perform all standard movements, as well as enter and exit the car. Driving and interactions are physics-driven for a realistic feel, giving full control over both the character and vehicle in a dynamic world.
