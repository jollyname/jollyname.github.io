C++ Bullet Physics

Download

A C++ game using Bullet Physics. Walk in third-person, enter and drive a car, and explore procedurally generated terrain, all from a simple main menu.

Controls:
Move: WASD
Jump: Space
Enter/Exit car: E
Toggle Cursor: F
Reset Car: R (only works when driving)
